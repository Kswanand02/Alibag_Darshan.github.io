Explore the special dishes in Alibag

Delight your taste buds in Alibag as you embark on a culinary adventure to explore the special dishes that define this coastal town. Renowned for its fresh seafood, Alibag offers a tempting array of dishes, including flavorful fish curries and crispy fried delights. Beyond seafood, indulge in the local charm with Maharashtrian favorites like fragrant poha and savory vada pav. Alibag's culinary scene is a celebration of diverse flavors, making it a must-visit destination for food enthusiasts eager to savor the unique and delicious offerings of this coastal paradise.
